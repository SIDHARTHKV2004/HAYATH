Palakkayam Thattu

Kannur, Kerala

Overview

Palakkayam Thattu is a picturesque hill station located in the Western Ghats near Kannur. Surrounded by dense forests and misty hills, it is a perfect destination for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts. The breathtaking view from the hilltop makes it a must-visit.

Best Time to Visit

September to March

Timings

6:00 AM – 6:00 PM

Entry Fee

₹50 per person

Travel Info

Palakkayam Thattu offers an offbeat experience with trekking paths, watch towers, and lush greenery all around. It's a popular weekend getaway for people living in and around Kannur. Adventure activities like zip-lining and off-road jeep rides are available.

  • Nearest Railway: Kannur Railway Station (50 km)
  • Nearest Airport: Kannur International Airport (45 km)
  • Nearby Attractions: Paithalmala, Alakapuri Waterfalls
