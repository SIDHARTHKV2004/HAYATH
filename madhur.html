Madhur Temple

Kasaragod, Kerala

Overview

Madhur Temple, also known as Madhur Mahaganapathi Temple, is a prominent Hindu pilgrimage site located on the banks of the Madhuvahini River. It is renowned for its unique three-tiered gajaprishta (elephant-back) architecture and the idol of Lord Ganesha that is believed to be growing in size over the years.

Best Time to Visit

October to April

Temple Timings

5:00 AM – 12:30 PM, 5:00 PM – 8:00 PM

Entry Fee

Free

History & Travel Info

The Madhur Mahaganapathi Temple has a history dating back centuries and is closely linked to the mythology of Lord Ganesha. Devotees flock here especially during the annual festival of Ganesh Chaturthi. The temple’s peaceful riverside location and serene surroundings make it a spiritually uplifting destination.

  • Nearest Railway: Kasaragod Railway Station (8 km)
  • Nearest Airport: Mangalore International Airport (60 km)
  • Nearby Attractions: Malik Deenar Mosque, Bekal Fort
