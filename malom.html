Malom

Coorg, Kerala

Overview

Malom, located in the beautiful district of Coorg, Kerala, is known for its lush greenery, coffee plantations, and scenic landscapes. It's the perfect destination for nature lovers and those looking to experience the tranquil beauty of Kerala's hill stations.

Best Time to Visit

November to February

Timings

Open all day

Entry Fee

Free

History & Travel Info

Malom is a serene location, surrounded by coffee plantations and lush forests. It is a hill station known for its scenic beauty and is ideal for trekking, nature walks, and enjoying the cooler climate. The area is relatively untouched by commercial tourism, making it a peaceful getaway.

  • Nearest Railway: Virajpet (50 km)
  • Nearest Airport: Mangalore (160 km)
  • Nearby Attractions: Abbey Falls, Iruppu Falls
