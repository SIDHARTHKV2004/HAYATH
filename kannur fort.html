Kannur Fort

Kannur, Kerala

Overview

Kannur Fort, also known as St. Angelo Fort, is a historic fort located by the Arabian Sea in Kannur. Built in 1505 by the Portuguese, the fort offers scenic coastal views, ancient architecture, and a glimpse into Kerala’s colonial past.

Best Time to Visit

October to March

Visiting Hours

8:00 AM – 6:00 PM

Entry Fee

₹25 (Indian Citizens), ₹300 (Foreign Nationals)

History & Travel Info

St. Angelo Fort has changed hands from Portuguese to Dutch and later to the British, playing a key role in Kerala's maritime history. Today, the fort is maintained by the Archaeological Survey of India and is a popular tourist destination.

  • Nearest Railway: Kannur Railway Station (3 km)
  • Nearest Airport: Kannur International Airport (28 km)
  • Nearby Attractions: Payyambalam Beach, Arakkal Museum
