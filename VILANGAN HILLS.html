Vilangan Hills

Thrissur, Kerala

Overview

Vilangan Hills is a popular eco-tourism destination located near Thrissur city. Often referred to as the "Oxygen Jar of Thrissur," it offers panoramic views, lush greenery, and peaceful surroundings. The hill is ideal for nature walks, picnics, and enjoying spectacular sunsets, making it a favorite among locals and tourists alike.

Best Time to Visit

October to March

Visiting Hours

7:00 AM – 7:00 PM

Entry Fee

Free

History & Travel Info

Vilangan Hills is known for its serene environment and was once used as a military base during World War II. Today, it's developed as a bio-park and recreational area with walkways, gardens, and an amphitheater. It provides an excellent escape from the city hustle, especially for families and fitness lovers.

  • Nearest Railway: Thrissur Railway Station (8 km)
  • Nearest Airport: Cochin International Airport (58 km)
  • Nearby Attractions: Shakthan Thampuran Palace, Thrissur Zoo, Vadakkumnathan Temple
