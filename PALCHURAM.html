Palchuram Waterfall

Kannur, Kerala

Overview

Palchuram Waterfall is a stunning natural wonder located in the Western Ghats of Kannur. Surrounded by lush forests, this waterfall is a hidden gem and a perfect getaway for those seeking tranquility and natural beauty. The cascading waters offer a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of daily life.

Best Time to Visit

June to September (Monsoon Season)

Timings

6:00 AM – 6:00 PM

Entry Fee

Free

Travel Info

Palchuram Waterfall is not only famous for its beauty but also for the trek that leads visitors through a dense, unspoiled forest. The journey to the waterfall offers an unforgettable experience with its picturesque views and tranquility. The nearby area is also known for its biodiversity and rich flora and fauna.

  • Nearest Railway: Kannur Railway Station (45 km)
  • Nearest Airport: Kannur International Airport (50 km)
  • Nearby Attractions: Iruvanjippuzha, Ezhimala
