Phantom Rock

Wayanad, Kerala

Overview

Phantom Rock is a unique archaeological site in Wayanad, named for its skull-shaped appearance that resembles a phantom. Perched at an elevation of 2,600 feet above sea level, it’s a popular trekking and photography spot, offering panoramic views of the surrounding hills and forests.

Best Time to Visit

October to March

Entry Timings

6:00 AM – 6:00 PM

Entry Fee

Free

History & Travel Info

This natural rock formation is located near Ambalavayal in Wayanad and is surrounded by lush greenery and ancient tribal areas. The trek to Phantom Rock is moderately easy and rewards visitors with awe-inspiring landscapes and a peaceful environment away from the hustle and bustle.

  • Nearest Railway: Kozhikode Railway Station (90 km)
  • Nearest Airport: Calicut International Airport (100 km)
  • Nearby Attractions: Edakkal Caves, Karapuzha Dam, Cheengeri Hills
