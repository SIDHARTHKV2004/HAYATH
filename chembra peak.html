Chembra Peak

Wayanad, Kerala

Overview

Chembra Peak, the highest point in Wayanad, offers a breathtaking trekking experience through lush green forests and grasslands. Known for the heart-shaped lake en route, this spot is perfect for adventure lovers and nature seekers.

Best Time to Visit

September to March

Timings

7:00 AM – 4:00 PM

Entry Fee

₹750 for group of 10 (trekking permit)

History & Travel Info

Chembra Peak stands at an elevation of 2,100 meters and is a popular trekking destination in the Western Ghats. The trek is moderately challenging and leads to the famous heart-shaped lake, which is believed to never dry up. Permission from the forest office is required before trekking.

  • Nearest Railway: Kozhikode Railway Station (about 79 km)
  • Nearest Airport: Calicut International Airport (about 92 km)
  • Nearby Attractions: Soochipara Falls, Pookode Lake, Edakkal Caves
