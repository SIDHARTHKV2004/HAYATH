Parassini Temple

Kannur, Kerala

Overview

Parassini Madappura Sree Muthappan Temple is a renowned religious and cultural landmark in Kannur. Dedicated to Lord Muthappan, this temple uniquely blends Hindu traditions with folk beliefs and welcomes people of all castes and religions.

Best Time to Visit

November to March

Temple Timings

5:00 AM – 8:00 PM

Entry Fee

Free

History & Travel Info

The temple is famous for its daily Theyyam performances, rituals, and free meals (prasadam) for devotees. Located on the banks of the Valapattanam River, Parassini offers spiritual calm and scenic views. The temple's origins are linked with local myths and oral traditions.

  • Nearest Railway: Kannur Railway Station (20 km)
  • Nearest Airport: Kannur International Airport (45 km)
  • Nearby Attractions: Vismaya Water Park, Pazhassi Dam
