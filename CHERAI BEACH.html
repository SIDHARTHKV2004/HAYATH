Cherai Beach

Ernakulam, Kerala

Overview

Cherai Beach is a beautiful beach located on the northern side of Vypin Island in Ernakulam district. Known for its long sandy shores, gentle waves, and proximity to the backwaters, it is an ideal spot for beach lovers and families. It's one of the few places in Kerala where you can see the sea and backwaters separated by just a narrow stretch of land.

Best Time to Visit

November to February

Activities

Swimming, Sunbathing, Dolphin Watching

Entry Fee

Free

Travel Info

Cherai Beach offers a peaceful and scenic environment with coconut groves and Chinese fishing nets nearby. The beach is well-maintained and popular among both locals and tourists. Resorts, homestays, and seafood restaurants add to the vibrant charm of this coastal getaway.

  • Nearest Railway: Ernakulam Junction (25 km)
  • Nearest Airport: Cochin International Airport (30 km)
  • Nearby Attractions: Pallippuram Fort, Munambam Beach, Kuzhupilly Beach
