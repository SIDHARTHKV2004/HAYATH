Kozhippara Waterfalls

Wayanad, Kerala

Overview

Kozhippara Waterfalls, located in the serene hills of Wayanad, Kerala, is a stunning natural attraction surrounded by dense forests and picturesque landscapes. The waterfall is known for its beauty and tranquility, making it a perfect spot for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Best Time to Visit

October to May

Timings

6:00 AM – 6:00 PM

Entry Fee

Free

History & Travel Info

Kozhippara Waterfalls is a popular tourist destination in Wayanad, nestled among the lush green forests of the Western Ghats. The falls cascade from a height, creating a mesmerizing sight. The area around the falls is ideal for trekking, photography, and picnics, making it a perfect getaway.

  • Nearest Railway: Kalpetta Railway Station (15 km)
  • Nearest Airport: Calicut International Airport (60 km)
  • Nearby Attractions: Edakkal Caves, Soochipara Waterfalls
