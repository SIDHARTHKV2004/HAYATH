Kappad Beach

Kozhikkode, Kerala

Overview

Kappad Beach is a historic beach located in Kozhikkode, Kerala. It is famous for being the landing point of Vasco da Gama in 1498, marking the beginning of the European influence in India. The beach is known for its calm waters, scenic beauty, and historical significance.

Best Time to Visit

November to March

Timings

Open 24 Hours

Entry Fee

Free

History & Travel Info

Kappad Beach is historically significant as it was here that Vasco da Gama first landed in 1498, opening the sea route to India. The beach is not only a place for relaxation but also a place of historical importance, with a stone memorial built to commemorate this historic event.

  • Nearest Railway: Kozhikkode Railway Station (16 km)
  • Nearest Airport: Calicut International Airport (25 km)
  • Nearby Attractions: Kozhikkode Beach, Mananchira Square
