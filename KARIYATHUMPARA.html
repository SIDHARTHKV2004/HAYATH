Kariyathumpara

Wayanad, Kerala

Overview

Kariyathumpara is a picturesque viewpoint located in Wayanad, Kerala. This hidden gem is known for its panoramic views of lush greenery, valleys, and the surrounding hills. A popular spot for nature lovers and photography enthusiasts, Kariyathumpara offers a serene atmosphere perfect for a peaceful getaway.

Best Time to Visit

October to May

Timings

6:00 AM – 6:00 PM

Entry Fee

Free

History & Travel Info

Kariyathumpara is a tranquil spot in Wayanad that offers stunning views of the Western Ghats. The place is less commercialized, making it an ideal destination for those seeking solitude. The lush environment surrounding the viewpoint adds to its charm, and it's perfect for trekking, nature walks, and relaxing in nature.

  • Nearest Railway: Kalpetta Railway Station (20 km)
  • Nearest Airport: Calicut International Airport (65 km)
  • Nearby Attractions: Edakkal Caves, Pookode Lake
