Sree Vadakkumnathan Temple

Thrissur, Kerala

Overview

Sree Vadakkumnathan Temple, located in the heart of Thrissur city, is one of the oldest and most revered temples in Kerala. Dedicated to Lord Shiva, the temple is known for its classic Kerala-style architecture, massive murals, and historical importance. It is also the focal point of the famous Thrissur Pooram festival.

Best Time to Visit

October to March

Temple Timings

3:00 AM – 10:30 AM, 5:00 PM – 8:30 PM

Entry Fee

Free (Only Hindus allowed inside)

History & Travel Info

Believed to be established by Parashurama, the temple is a classic representation of Kerala’s spiritual and architectural heritage. The temple complex covers nine acres and is surrounded by a stone wall with monumental towers on all four sides. The annual Thrissur Pooram attracts thousands of devotees and tourists from around the world.

  • Nearest Railway: Thrissur Railway Station (2 km)
  • Nearest Airport: Cochin International Airport (55 km)
  • Nearby Attractions: Shakthan Thampuran Palace, Thrissur Zoo, Paramekkavu Temple
