Neelimala View Point

Wayanad, Kerala

Overview

Neelimala View Point is a hidden gem in Wayanad offering panoramic views of lush valleys and majestic waterfalls. It’s a paradise for nature lovers and adventure seekers, with misty mountain air and peaceful trekking paths.

Best Time to Visit

October to February

Timings

6:00 AM – 6:00 PM

Entry Fee

Free (Guide services may cost extra)

History & Travel Info

Located near Vaduvanchal, Neelimala View Point is known for its scenic trekking trail that leads you through coffee plantations and misty forests. From the summit, you get a stunning view of Meenmutty Waterfalls cascading through the greenery.

  • Nearest Railway: Kozhikode Railway Station (about 90 km)
  • Nearest Airport: Calicut International Airport (about 105 km)
  • Nearby Attractions: Meenmutty Falls, Chembra Peak, Edakkal Caves
