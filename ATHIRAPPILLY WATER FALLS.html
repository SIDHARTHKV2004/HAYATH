Athirappilly Waterfalls

Thrissur, Kerala

Overview

Athirappilly Waterfalls, often called the "Niagara of India," is the largest waterfall in Kerala and a must-visit destination for nature lovers. The waterfall cascades from a height of 80 feet, surrounded by lush greenery and the Sholayar forest. It is a popular filming location and a favorite among tourists for its majestic beauty.

Best Time to Visit

June to October (monsoon season)

Visiting Hours

8:00 AM – 6:00 PM

Entry Fee

₹30 (Adults), ₹10 (Children)

History & Travel Info

Athirappilly is part of the Chalakudy River and is surrounded by a diverse ecosystem with rich flora and fauna. It’s not just a sightseeing spot but also home to rare and endangered species like the Great Hornbill. The waterfall is accessible via a short trek through the forest, offering a close-up view of its thunderous drop.

  • Nearest Railway: Chalakudy Railway Station (30 km)
  • Nearest Airport: Cochin International Airport (40 km)
  • Nearby Attractions: Vazhachal Falls, Sholayar Dam, Charpa Falls
