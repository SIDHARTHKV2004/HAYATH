Vismaya Water Park

Kannur, Kerala

Overview

Vismaya Water Park, located near Parassinikadavu in Kannur, is a fun-filled amusement destination perfect for families and thrill-seekers. With water rides, pools, and entertainment for all ages, it’s a refreshing break from the ordinary.

Best Time to Visit

October to March

Park Timings

11:00 AM – 6:00 PM (Closed on Mondays)

Entry Fee

₹500 for adults, ₹400 for children

About & Travel Info

Operated by the Malabar Tourism Development Cooperative, Vismaya offers various water rides, rain dance, virtual reality games, and food courts. It's well-maintained and known for its cleanliness and family-friendly atmosphere.

  • Nearest Railway: Kannur Railway Station (20 km)
  • Nearest Airport: Kannur International Airport (40 km)
  • Nearby Attractions: Parassini Temple, Pazhassi Dam
