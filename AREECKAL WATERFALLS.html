Areeckal Waterfalls

Ernakulam, Kerala

Overview

Areeckal Waterfalls is a hidden gem located near Piravom in the Ernakulam district. Surrounded by lush greenery and rocky terrain, this charming waterfall cascades from a moderate height and offers a serene environment for nature lovers and photographers. It’s an ideal day-trip destination for those looking to escape the city.

Best Time to Visit

June to November (Monsoon/Post-Monsoon)

Timings

6:00 AM – 6:00 PM

Entry Fee

Free

History & Travel Info

Areeckal Waterfalls gained popularity in recent years as more travelers began exploring local hidden spots. Though not commercialized, the area has basic facilities and a few viewpoints. It is located amidst rubber plantations and requires a short walk through scenic trails to reach the falls.

  • Nearest Railway: Piravom Road Railway Station (12 km)
  • Nearest Airport: Cochin International Airport (50 km)
  • Nearby Attractions: Piravom Valiya Palli, Illithode Forest Trail, Areekkal Caves
