Ananthapuram Lake Temple

Kasaragod, Kerala

Overview

Ananthapuram Lake Temple, located in the picturesque Kasaragod district of Kerala, is a serene temple surrounded by a beautiful lake. The temple is dedicated to Lord Vishnu and is the only temple in Kerala located on the banks of a lake.

Best Time to Visit

October to March

Timings

6:00 AM – 8:00 PM

Entry Fee

Free

History & Travel Info

The temple is believed to be the place where Lord Vishnu is worshipped in his eternal form, and it is steeped in history. It is said that the temple was established by a saint called "Naranathu Bhranthan," who meditated here. The tranquil surroundings and the holy lake attract numerous pilgrims and tourists every year.

  • Nearest Railway: Kasaragod Railway Station (20 km)
  • Nearest Airport: Mangalore (around 70 km)
  • Nearby Attractions: Bekal Fort, Kappil Beach
