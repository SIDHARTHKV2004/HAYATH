Bekal Fort

Kasaragod, Kerala

Overview

Bekal Fort, a majestic structure by the Arabian Sea, is the largest fort in Kerala. Built in the 17th century, it offers breathtaking sea views, historic architecture, and a serene ambiance.

Best Time to Visit

October to March

Timings

8:00 AM – 5:30 PM

Entry Fee

₹20 (Indians), ₹100 (Foreigners)

History & Travel Info

Bekal Fort was built by Shivappa Nayaka of the Keladi Nayaka dynasty in the 1650s. Spanning 40 acres, the fort's keyhole shape and advanced defense features make it architecturally unique. The observation tower offers panoramic sea views, and visitors can explore ancient temples and hidden tunnels within.

  • Nearest Railway: Bekal Fort Station (2 km)
  • Nearest Airport: Mangalore (around 60 km)
  • Nearby Attractions: Chandragiri Fort, Kappil Beach
