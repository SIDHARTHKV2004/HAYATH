Sweet Market (Mittayi Theruvu)

Kozhikode, Kerala

Overview

Mittayi Theruvu, also known as the Sweet Market, is a vibrant street located in Kozhikode, Kerala. Famous for its traditional sweets, this bustling street offers a delightful experience for those with a sweet tooth. The street is lined with various shops selling an assortment of mouth-watering local sweets like halwa, unniyappams, and more.

Best Time to Visit

October to March

Timings

Open 10:00 AM – 8:00 PM

Entry Fee

Free

History & Travel Info

The Sweet Market or Mittayi Theruvu has been a part of Kozhikode's heritage for many years. The market showcases the rich culinary tradition of Kerala with its vibrant street food culture and traditional sweets. Visitors can indulge in local delicacies, soak in the aromas of fresh sweets, and experience the charm of old Kozhikode.

  • Nearest Railway: Kozhikode Railway Station (3 km)
  • Nearest Airport: Calicut International Airport (28 km)
  • Nearby Attractions: Kozhikode Beach, Mananchira Square
