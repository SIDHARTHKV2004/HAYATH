Rani Puram

Kasaragod, Kerala

Overview

Rani Puram, a serene hill station in Kasaragod, Kerala, offers breathtaking views of lush greenery, misty hills, and cool weather. Ideal for nature lovers and adventure seekers, Rani Puram is a perfect retreat away from the bustle of city life.

Best Time to Visit

October to March

Timings

Open 24 Hours

Entry Fee

Free

History & Travel Info

Rani Puram is a tranquil hill station known for its scenic views, tranquil atmosphere, and rich biodiversity. The region is surrounded by dense forests, waterfalls, and streams, making it a popular destination for trekkers and nature enthusiasts.

  • Nearest Railway: Kasaragod Station (around 40 km)
  • Nearest Airport: Mangalore International Airport (around 100 km)
  • Nearby Attractions: Kottanchery Hills, Malik Deenar Mosque
