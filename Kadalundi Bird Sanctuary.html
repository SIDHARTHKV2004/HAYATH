Kadalundi Bird Sanctuary

Kozhikode, Kerala

Overview

Kadalundi Bird Sanctuary is a haven for bird lovers and nature enthusiasts, located near the Kadalundi River in Kozhikode, Kerala. The sanctuary is home to a wide variety of migratory and native birds, particularly during the winter season. It provides a peaceful environment for birdwatching amidst lush greenery and tranquil backwaters.

Best Time to Visit

November to March

Timings

6:00 AM – 6:00 PM

Entry Fee

Free

History & Travel Info

The Kadalundi Bird Sanctuary, established in 1983, covers an area of around 3.5 square kilometers and is situated on the banks of the Kadalundi River. The sanctuary attracts thousands of migratory birds from different parts of the world, making it a popular spot for birdwatching. In addition to birdlife, the sanctuary is rich in flora and fauna and offers beautiful views of the river and surrounding landscape.

  • Nearest Railway: Kadalundi Railway Station (2 km)
  • Nearest Airport: Calicut International Airport (25 km)
  • Nearby Attractions: Kappad Beach, Kozhikode Beach
